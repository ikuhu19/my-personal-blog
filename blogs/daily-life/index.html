🌈 Life in Moods, Not Timetables

Some people have timetables. I have moods. I’ve never made one in my life – not because I’m careless, but because I know myself. If I vibe with it, I’ll sit for hours until it’s done. If not… well, it can sit there forever. For other necessary things we have deadlines. 💀

One day, I’m all sunshine – feeling blissful, counting blessings, grateful for the amazing people around me. The next, I’m questioning why basic decency isn’t common sense and why kids sometimes seem to have higher IQ than adults. Then, as usual, I end up laughing at myself for even caring so much (tsk tsk 😪) Lesson learned… until next time.

Eighteen years on Earth (nineteen ongoing 💅) and I swear I’ve already met the full catalogue of people – not just in college but literally everywhere. Some make me smile, some test my patience, but all of them? They make life way more entertaining. Mind you, all sometimes you irritate me till my soul says stop 💢. Although I'm happy that I am not very nosy (Am I? 😗).

🛋 My Detox Strategy

Now back to my favourite task? Being unproductive for a whole day (with valid reasons 😩). Why? Because the moment something threatens my peace or starts draining my energy, I instantly start analysing how to detox from it and how to adjust around it for better peace of mind.

People say, “Just ignore it.” My friends say that too. But deep down? It’s not that simple. Things affect me. So what do I do in such situations? Simple – I find a solution. Even if it feels like there’s nothing I can do. I take my time, decide my outcome, and once I find my path, it’s over. Next time someone mentions it – situation, person, or problem – I feel nothing. Hands off. It works, trust me. Don’t ignore. Decide.
