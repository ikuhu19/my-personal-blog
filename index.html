Welcome to Pieces of Kuhu

This isn’t just another corner of the internet. It’s my journal in disguise, a safe place where I spill the things we often keep tucked away — the late-night thoughts, the quiet battles, the tiny joys that never make it to Instagram stories. Here, you’ll find raw honesty stitched with hope. Stories of healing that remind us we’re all a little broken, yet somehow still whole. Reflections on daily life — messy, funny, confusing, but real. My dream is to make you feel seen. To give you words you didn’t know you were looking for, and to create a space where you pause, breathe, and whisper, “Yes, this is exactly how I feel.” So, welcome. Take what you need. Leave with a lighter heart.

My Journey

Life doesn’t come with a map, and I’m still finding my way through its twists and turns. Some days feel light and effortless, others heavy and confusing — yet every moment teaches, nudges, and shapes us. Here, I write about the little things that matter: the quiet mornings, the laughter that sneaks in, the doubts that linger, and the tiny victories that make you pause and smile. Connect with me on this journey. Together we fall, we grow, we explore, and we discover. Together we notice the hidden pieces of life we often overlook, and maybe, as you read, you’ll see fragments of your own story reflected in mine.
